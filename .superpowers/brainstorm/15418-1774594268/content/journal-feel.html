The Flooded Cathedral

I was standing in a cathedral, but the floor was covered in dark water up to my ankles. The stained glass windows were still glowing even though there was no light outside. Each window showed a different face — people I knew but couldn't name.

I tried to walk toward the altar but the water kept rising. It wasn't cold. It felt like it was trying to tell me something. There was singing coming from below the water.

Interpretation

The flooded cathedral suggests sacred aspects of your psyche that are being submerged by unconscious material. The rising water isn't threatening — it's the unconscious asking to be acknowledged.

The faces you recognize but can't name may be aspects of your own personality you haven't yet integrated...
