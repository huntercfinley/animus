A. White Cards + Mood Border

Clean white cards with soft shadows. Mood shown as a thin colored left border. Minimal, scannable, like a premium journal app. Mood color is an accent, not the star.

B. Mood-Tinted Cards

Each card has a very faint wash of its mood color — pale blue for peaceful, pale rose for anxious, pale gold for joyful. Scrolling through the journal reveals your emotional landscape at a glance. More expressive.
