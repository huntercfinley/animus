A. Three Layers

Surface (cloud-white) → Twilight (lavender) → Deep (indigo). Each screen lives at a fixed depth. Clean transitions when navigating between layers.

B. Continuous Gradient

Smooth, unbroken spectrum from cloud-white to deep indigo. Every screen sits at a specific point on the gradient. Backgrounds shift subtly as you scroll or navigate deeper.

C. Surface + Dive (Recommended)

Most of the app is bright and airy. Only "Go Deeper" and Shadow Work pull you into the dark — it's an intentional, dramatic shift. Maximizes the bright identity while making the deep moments feel powerful.

Why I recommend C

The more of the app that's bright, the more it stands out in the App Store against every dark-purple competitor. And the contrast makes the "dive" moments genuinely dramatic — like the moment in a movie where the color palette shifts. Three layers (A) dilutes the bright identity. Continuous (B) is beautiful but makes the bright parts less bright by blending everything.
