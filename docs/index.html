Animus

Reveal what lies beneath.

You wake up gripping a feeling you can't name. Fragments — a flooded house, a stranger's face, falling through an endless corridor. Within minutes, they dissolve.

Animus catches them.

Speak your dream the moment you open your eyes. Animus transcribes, refines, and interprets it using the framework of Carl Jung — the psychologist who spent a lifetime mapping the language of the unconscious. Every interpretation is shaped by your life: the people in your world, the events on your mind, the symbols that keep returning night after night.

What Animus does

  • Record by voice. Capture dreams as you wake, polished into a beautifully written journal entry.
  • Personalized interpretations. Build your inner world inside Animus — people, places, themes — and watch the AI weave that context into every reading.
  • Go deeper. Have a conversation about any dream. Ask follow-up questions. Challenge the interpretation.
  • Dream artwork. Every dream generates a unique painting matched to its mood — surrealist, dark fantasy, watercolor, or beyond.
  • The Dream Web. Your unconscious as a constellation. Symbols, archetypes, and connections emerge over time.
  • Shadow work. Personalized exercises drawn from your dream symbols — a bridge between insight and inner work.
