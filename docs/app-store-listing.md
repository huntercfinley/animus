## Description (4000 char max)

You wake up gripping a feeling you can't name. Fragments — a flooded house, a stranger's face, falling through an endless corridor. Within minutes, they dissolve.

Animus catches them.

Speak your dream the moment you open your eyes. Animus transcribes, refines, and interprets it using the framework of Carl Jung — the psychologist who spent a lifetime mapping the language of the unconscious.

This is not a dream dictionary. There is no generic list of "what water means." Every interpretation is shaped by your life — the people in your world, the events on your mind, the symbols that keep returning night after night.

RECORD IN SECONDS
Tap and speak. Animus uses voice recognition to capture your dream in real time, then polishes the raw transcript into a beautifully written journal entry. No typing, no friction — just the space between sleep and waking.

INTERPRETATIONS THAT KNOW YOU
Build your personal world inside Animus: the people, places, and themes that matter to you. The AI weaves this context into every interpretation, surfacing connections a generic tool never could.

GO DEEPER
Not satisfied with the surface? Have a conversation with the AI about any dream. Ask follow-up questions. Challenge the interpretation. Explore what a specific symbol means for you. It remembers the full context.

DREAM ARTWORK
Every dream generates a unique piece of art — matched to the mood and content of what you experienced. Surrealist oil paintings for the strange. Dark fantasy for nightmares. Soft watercolor for the peaceful. Your journal becomes a gallery.

THE DREAM WEB
Watch your unconscious mind take shape. The Dream Web maps every dream as a point of light in a constellation, clustered by archetype. Over weeks and months, patterns emerge — recurring symbols, shadow elements, the threads connecting dreams you thought were unrelated.

SHADOW WORK
Jung believed the parts of ourselves we deny don't disappear — they show up in dreams. Animus generates personalized shadow work exercises drawn from your dream symbols, creating a bridge between insight and inner work.

SHARE YOUR DREAMS
Every dream becomes a shareable card — a branded image with your dream's artwork, title, and mood. Post to Instagram, send to a friend, or keep it for yourself.

BUILT FOR HONESTY
Every feature is accessible on the free tier. Ads are shown transparently — we tell you exactly why they're there. Premium removes ads and unlocks deeper analysis: pattern reports, archetype profiles, audio playback, and extended conversations.
