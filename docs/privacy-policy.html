Privacy Policy

Animus: Dream Journal — Reality Suites LLC — Effective April 3, 2026

Animus is a dream journaling and interpretation app built by Reality Suites. We take your privacy seriously, especially given the personal nature of dream content. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (which is hashed and never stored in plain text).

Dream Journal Entries. When you record a dream, we collect the voice transcript, the polished journal text, your mood selection, and any lucidity rating you provide. For premium subscribers, we also store the original audio recording.

Personal Context ("My World"). You may optionally add information about people, places, themes, and life events that are meaningful to you. This context is used solely to improve the relevance of your dream interpretations.

Conversations & Exercises. If you use the "Go Deeper" feature or Shadow Work exercises, we store your questions, responses, and the AI-generated replies.

Usage Data. We track basic feature usage counts (e.g., how many times you've used a feature today) to enforce free-tier limits. We do not use third-party analytics services.

Device Information. If you are a free-tier user and see ads, Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

How We Use Your Data

  • Dream Interpretation. Your dream text and personal context are sent to Anthropic (Claude) to generate Jungian-style interpretations, symbol analysis, and therapeutic insights.
  • Image Generation. A short image prompt derived from your dream is sent to Google (Gemini or Imagen) to generate dream artwork. The prompt does not include your full dream text.
  • Subscriptions. Purchase and subscription status is managed by RevenueCat and processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not directly handle payment information.
  • Advertising. Free-tier users may see ads served by Google AdMob. Premium subscribers see no ads.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Cloud database and authentication. Stores all user data with row-level security (each user can only access their own data).
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI dream interpretation and analysis. Receives dream text and personal context to generate responses.
  • Google AI (Gemini / Imagen) — Image generation from dream-derived prompts.
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. Receives your user ID and purchase status.
  • Google AdMob — Ad serving for free-tier users. May collect device identifiers.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking beyond what is described above.
  • We do not share your dream content with other users.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models. Third-party AI providers process your data according to their own API terms of service.

Data Security

All data is transmitted over TLS encryption. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's native secure storage (iOS Keychain or Android Keystore). Our database enforces row-level security, meaning your data is only accessible to your authenticated account.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete individual dream entries at any time. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email below. Upon account deletion, all personal data, including dream entries, audio recordings, and personal context, will be permanently removed from our systems.

Children's Privacy

Animus is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. You may also opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
