Common questions

My dream didn't save

Animus saves dreams to the cloud after interpretation. If you saw a "saved locally" message, it means you were briefly offline — Animus queues the dream and processes it the next time you have a connection. Open the app on Wi-Fi or cellular and it will sync automatically.

Image generation failed

Image generation can occasionally fail because of content moderation filters or transient API issues. Open the dream and tap Retry image. If it keeps failing, the prompt may have triggered a safety filter — try shortening or rewording the dream's most violent or sensitive parts and re-record.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Animus → Cancel. Cancelling preserves access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Open Animus → Settings → tap Delete Account at the bottom. This permanently deletes your dreams, profile, and all associated data from our servers. The action is irreversible.

Is my dream content private?

Yes. Dreams are encrypted in transit and at rest, stored under row-level access controls so only you can see your own data, and never sold. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Voice recording isn't picking up audio

Open iOS Settings → Animus → and ensure Microphone and Speech Recognition are both enabled. If they are and the issue persists, restart the app.

Why does Animus show ads?

The free tier is supported by ads so everyone can use the core features regardless of budget. Ads only appear at clear, predictable points (after dream interpretation, when generating an image without Lumen). Upgrade to Premium to remove ads entirely.

Premium subscription

Animus Premium is $4.99/month or $35.99/year. Premium removes ads, unlocks longer interpretations, gives you Imagen 4 Standard quality images, and adds extended Go Deeper conversations.
